I am Yi Zhang (张艺), a third-year Ph.D. student at the Warwick Manufacturing Group (WMG), University of Warwick. I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Xingyu Zhao and Prof. Siddartha Khastgir. Before beginning my Ph.D. journey, I received both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Computer Science from Beijing Language and Culture University (BLCU). My Master's research focused on Natural Language Processing (NLP), particularly on knowledge graphs and semantic understanding, under the supervision of Prof. Endong Xun.

My current research interests include:

  • Probabilistic Robustness
  • Safe and Reliable Generative Models
  • Vision-Language Foundation Models
  • Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence (AI)
